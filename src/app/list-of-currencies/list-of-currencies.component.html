The US dollar (denoted by the $ or USD sign) is the national currency of the United States of America. One dollar is equal to 100 cents.

Euro (denoted by the sign €, or EUR) is the official single currency of the European Union. One euro is equal to 100 cents (the name "euro cents" is also used).

The Russian ruble is the national currency of the Russian Federation (denoted by the RUB sign and the ₽ symbol).
